University Project: Solar Park Monitoring

Project Overview

The solar park monitoring project at our university aims to develop a comprehensive system to monitor and analyze the performance of a solar park. The project involves collecting real-time data from solar panels, weather sensors, and other relevant parameters to provide insights and optimize the park's efficiency.

Project Goals

  • Monitor and analyze solar panel performance
  • Track environmental factors impacting solar park efficiency
  • Develop a user-friendly dashboard for data visualization
  • Implement alerts and notifications for system anomalies
  • Optimize solar park operations for maximum energy production

Project Progress

Our team has made significant progress in designing and implementing the solar park monitoring system. We have successfully integrated data collection from various sensors and developed a web-based dashboard for visualizing the collected data. The system is currently undergoing testing and refinement to ensure accurate and reliable results.
